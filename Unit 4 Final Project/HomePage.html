About the Event

The Academy Gala is considered the most prestigious and glamorous fashion event in the world. It's a night where people with high academic achievements showcase their creativity based on a theme.

Our Organization

The Academy Gala is a high-fashion fundraiser that opens the Institute's annual fashion exhibit. After the gala, the exhibit is open to the public.
